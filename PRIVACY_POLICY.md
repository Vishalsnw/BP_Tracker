# Privacy Policy for VitalFlow

**Last Updated: December 20, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

VitalFlow ("the App," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Health Data
- Blood pressure readings (systolic and diastolic values)
- Pulse rate measurements
- Weight entries
- Glucose readings
- Measurement timestamps and notes
- Medication information and schedules
- Health goals and reminders

### 2.2 Personal Information
- User profile information (name, age, gender)
- Contact information (email address)
- Device information (device type, operating system version)
- App usage analytics

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information
- App crash reports and performance metrics
- Feature usage patterns
- Session duration and frequency

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Primary Use**: To provide core functionality of the App including tracking blood pressure, glucose, weight, and medication management
- **Health Insights**: To generate personalized health insights, trends, and recommendations
- **Reminders & Notifications**: To send measurement reminders and health alerts
- **Export & Reporting**: To generate PDF reports for sharing with healthcare providers
- **App Improvement**: To enhance App features, fix bugs, and improve user experience
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with the App
- **Communication**: To send important updates about the App
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All health data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted local database
- Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly export or backup it

### 4.2 Security Measures
- We implement industry-standard encryption protocols for all data
- Sensitive data is encrypted both at rest and in transit
- We regularly review and update our security practices
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Health data is retained on your device indefinitely until you delete it
- You can delete individual readings or all data at any time through the App settings
- Deleted data cannot be recovered

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Health Connect Integration
- The App may integrate with Android Health Connect with your explicit permission
- Health Connect allows you to share data with other health apps
- You control what data is shared through Health Connect settings
- Health Connect data sharing follows Google's privacy policies

### 5.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)
- Cloud backup features (if available) are optional and require your explicit consent
- You can disable cloud synchronization at any time
- Backup services use encryption to protect your data in transit and storage

### 5.3 Analytics & Crash Reporting
- We may use third-party analytics tools to understand App usage patterns
- These tools may collect anonymized usage data
- Crash reports help us identify and fix App issues

## 6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 6.1 What We Don't Share
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your health information to third parties
- We do not share your personal information with advertisers
- We do not use your health data for targeted marketing

### 6.2 When We May Share Data
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or government request
- **Service Providers**: With trusted vendors who help us operate the App (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- **Your Consent**: When you explicitly choose to share data (e.g., exporting to healthcare provider)
- **Health Connect**: Data shared through Health Connect per your settings

## 7. User Rights & Control

You have the right to:
- Access all your stored health data
- Export your data in standard formats (CSV, PDF)
- Delete any or all of your data
- Disable notifications and reminders
- Control Health Connect sharing permissions
- Opt-out of optional analytics

## 8. Children's Privacy

VitalFlow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information.

For users between 13-18, parental consent is recommended before using the App.

## 9. International Users

If you are accessing VitalFlow from outside the United States, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries where we operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfer, storage, and processing.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the updated policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date above
- Requesting your consent if required by law

Your continued use of the App following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept and agree to the changes.

## 11. Medical Disclaimer

**IMPORTANT**: VitalFlow is designed for informational purposes and personal tracking only. It is NOT a medical device and should NOT be used:
- To diagnose medical conditions
- As a substitute for professional medical advice
- In place of consultation with licensed healthcare providers
- For emergency situations (call emergency services immediately)

Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional regarding your health and any concerns about your blood pressure or other vital signs.
